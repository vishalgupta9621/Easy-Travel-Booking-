## Getting Started

### System Requirements
- Modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
- Internet connection
- JavaScript enabled
- Cookies enabled

### Accessing the System
1. Open your web browser
2. Navigate to the Travel Booking System URL
3. You'll see the homepage with search options for flights, trains, and buses

### First Time Users
1. Click "Register" in the top navigation
2. Fill in your personal details
3. Verify your email address
4. Log in with your credentials

## Account Management

### Creating an Account
1. **Click "Register"** in the navigation bar
2. **Fill in required information:**
   - Username (3-20 characters, letters, numbers, underscore only)
   - Email address (valid email required)
   - Password (minimum 6 characters with uppercase, lowercase, and number)
   - First and Last Name
   - Phone number (Indian mobile number format)
3. **Click "Create Account"**
4. **Check your email** for verification (if enabled)

### Logging In
1. **Click "Login"** in the navigation bar
2. **Enter your credentials:**
   - Username or Email
   - Password
3. **Click "Sign In"**
4. You'll be redirected to the homepage

### Profile Management
1. **Access your profile** by clicking your name in the navigation
2. **Update information:**
   - Personal details
   - Contact information
   - Travel preferences
   - Password change
3. **View loyalty points** and booking history

## Searching for Travel Options

### Flight Search
1. **Select "Flights" tab** on the homepage
2. **Choose trip type:**
   - One Way: Single journey
   - Round Trip: Return journey
3. **Enter travel details:**
   - **From:** Type city name or airport code (e.g., "Delhi" or "DEL")
   - **To:** Destination city or airport
   - **Departure Date:** Select from calendar
   - **Return Date:** (for round trips only)
   - **Passengers:** Number of travelers (1-9)
   - **Class:** Economy, Business, or First Class
4. **Click "Search Flights"**

### Train Search
1. **Select "Trains" tab**
2. **Enter journey details:**
   - **From:** Railway station name or code
   - **To:** Destination station
   - **Journey Date:** Select date
   - **Passengers:** Number of travelers
   - **Class:** SL (Sleeper), 3A (3rd AC), 2A (2nd AC), 1A (1st AC), CC (Chair Car)
3. **Click "Search Trains"**

### Bus Search
1. **Select "Buses" tab**
2. **Enter travel information:**
   - **From:** Bus station or city
   - **To:** Destination
   - **Travel Date:** Select date
   - **Passengers:** Number of travelers
   - **Bus Type:** AC Seater, Non-AC Seater, AC Sleeper, etc.
3. **Click "Search Buses"**

### Search Tips
- **Use autocomplete:** Start typing and select from suggestions
- **Flexible dates:** Try different dates for better prices
- **Compare options:** Check multiple travel modes
- **Book early:** Better availability and prices

## Search Results

### Understanding Results
Each result shows:
- **Service details:** Flight number, train name, bus operator
- **Timing:** Departure and arrival times
- **Duration:** Total travel time
- **Price:** Per person cost including taxes
- **Availability:** Remaining seats
- **Amenities:** Available services

### Filtering and Sorting
- **Sort by:** Price, Duration, Departure Time, Arrival Time
- **Filter by:** 
  - Price range (slider)
  - Departure time (Morning, Afternoon, Evening, Night)
  - Specific amenities or features
- **Results update automatically** as you apply filters

### Selecting Your Option
1. **Review details** of each option
2. **Click "View Details"** for more information
3. **Click "Book Now"** or "Select Seats" (for buses) to proceed

## Booking Process

### Step 1: Service Selection
- Choose your preferred flight, train, or bus
- Review timing, price, and terms
- Click "Book Now" to continue

### Step 2: Passenger Details
1. **Add passenger information** for each traveler:
   - Title (Mr, Ms, Mrs, Dr)
   - Full name (as per ID proof)
   - Age
   - Gender
   - Seat preference (Window, Aisle, Middle)
2. **For multiple passengers:** Click "Add Passenger"
3. **Review all details** for accuracy

### Step 3: Contact Information
1. **Primary contact details:**
   - Email address (for booking confirmation)
   - Phone number (for updates)
2. **Emergency contact** (optional but recommended):
   - Name and phone number

### Step 4: Seat Selection (Buses)
For bus bookings:
1. **View seat map** showing available seats
2. **Click on preferred seats** (green = available, red = booked)
3. **Confirm seat selection**

### Step 5: Review and Payment
1. **Review booking summary:**
   - Travel details
   - Passenger information
   - Total cost breakdown
2. **Select payment method**
3. **Proceed to payment**

## Payment Methods

### Available Payment Options
1. **Credit Card**
   - Visa, MasterCard, American Express
   - Enter card details securely
2. **Debit Card**
   - All major Indian banks supported
3. **UPI (Unified Payments Interface)**
   - PhonePe, Paytm, Google Pay, BHIM
   - Enter UPI ID
4. **Net Banking**
   - All major Indian banks
   - Redirected to bank's secure portal
5. **Digital Wallets**
   - Paytm, PhonePe, Amazon Pay

### Payment Security
- **SSL Encryption:** All transactions are secured
- **No card storage:** Card details are not saved
- **PCI Compliance:** Industry-standard security
- **OTP Verification:** Additional security layer

### Payment Process
1. **Select payment method**
2. **Enter payment details**
3. **Complete verification** (OTP, 3D Secure)
4. **Receive confirmation**

## Managing Bookings

### Viewing Your Bookings
1. **Log in to your account**
2. **Click "My Bookings"** in navigation
3. **View all bookings** with status:
   - Confirmed: Payment successful, booking confirmed
   - Pending: Awaiting payment or confirmation
   - Cancelled: Booking cancelled
   - Completed: Travel completed

### Booking Details
Click on any booking to view:
- Complete travel itinerary
- Passenger details
- Payment information
- Cancellation options
- Support contact

### Cancellation Process
1. **Open booking details**
2. **Click "Cancel Booking"**
3. **Select cancellation reason**
4. **Review cancellation charges:**
   - More than 72 hours: ₹100 flat fee
   - 24-72 hours: 25% of booking amount
   - 2-24 hours: 50% of booking amount
   - Less than 2 hours: No refund
5. **Confirm cancellation**
6. **Refund processed** to original payment method

### Modification Requests
- **Contact customer support** for modifications
- **Subject to availability** and fare difference
- **Modification charges** may apply
- **Not all bookings** are modifiable

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues and Solutions

#### Login Problems
- **Forgot password:** Use "Forgot Password" link
- **Account locked:** Contact customer support
- **Email not verified:** Check spam folder for verification email

#### Search Issues
- **No results found:** Try different dates or nearby locations
- **Slow loading:** Check internet connection, try refreshing
- **Error messages:** Clear browser cache and cookies

#### Booking Problems
- **Payment failed:** Check card details, try different payment method
- **Session timeout:** Complete booking within 15 minutes
- **Seat unavailable:** Refresh and select different seats

#### Technical Issues
- **Page not loading:** Clear browser cache, try different browser
- **Mobile app crashes:** Update app, restart device
- **Slow performance:** Check internet speed, close other applications

### Browser Compatibility
- **Recommended browsers:** Chrome 90+, Firefox 88+, Safari 14+, Edge 90+
- **Enable JavaScript and cookies**
- **Disable ad blockers** if experiencing issues
- **Update browser** to latest version
